Privacy Policy for String to CSV Converter

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to String to CSV Converter, a tool that converts strings into CSV format. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

We want to make it clear that our Chrome extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. All data processing is done locally on your device. We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Device information such as IP addresses, browser types, or operating systems.
  • Any of the text or IDs you enter into the extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
